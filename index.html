Jam session application for Android

Welcome to ElectroJam.

ElectroJam is an Android application which lets you create sample based music using your (Android based) smartphone and/or tablet device. You can create this music on a single device, or create a "jam session" with multiple devices connected to the same WiFi network.

Authors and Contributors

ElectroJam was created by David Jennes (@djbe) and Jean Patry (@jptr) during a course for development for mobile devices at Télécom ParisTech, taught by professor Samuel Tardieu (@samueltardieu). After we course we decided to continue developing this application and hope it might be of interest to other people.
